PRD ReDesign: Main Homepage

1. Background & Context

The homepage is the primary entry point for both new and returning users. With ~590K monthly landings (497K new, 110K returning), we must ensure a seamless and trustworthy user journey. Current analytics highlight strong engagement at the navigation tabs (69% reach), but weak depth of scroll (only 10% past "How it works") and a bounce rate of 16%. Conversion to paid orders within 24h is 7.31%.

The redesign must:

  • Improve clarity and usability for new visitors.
  • Reinforce trust and credibility for returning visitors.
  • Optimize conversion by improving navigation, product discoverability, and trust signals.

2. Personas

  • New Visitors (497K / month):
    • Likely arrive from marketing campaigns or organic search.
    • Priorities: trust, anonymity, security, fast delivery, easy navigation to relevant treatment.
  • Returning Visitors (110K / month):
    • Previously purchased or explored our services.
    • Need reassurance of consistent service and motivation to repurchase.

3. Success Metrics

  • Maintain or improve bounce rate (currently 16%, target ≤ 12%).
  • Fast page loading: Google considers <2.5s LCP as "Good" speed. Target Google PageSpeed score: ≥90.
  • Conversion: Improve from 7.31% to ≥8.04% (+10%) from homepage view → paid order within 24h.

4. Analytics Data (Last 30 Days)

  • Unique Users Landing: 590K
    • New site: 497K
    • Old site: 110K
  • Scroll depth:
    • 69% reach navigation tabs (categories).
    • 10% scroll past "How it works".
  • Bounce Rate: 16%
  • Conversion from view → paid order (24h windows): 7.31%
