PRD ReDesign: RX Category Page

1. Background & Context

The RX Category Page serves as the main gateway for users exploring prescription treatments on DoktorABC. It must communicate trust, professionalism, and simplicity while leading users efficiently toward starting a consultation.

Most users reach this page either organically (through Google searches for specific treatments) or from marketing campaigns. The page must help both new and returning visitors quickly understand who we are, how the process works, and why they should trust DoktorABC to handle their prescription safely and discreetly.

The design should reflect the same visual consistency as the homepage and other category pages - while giving the RX category a more medical, reliable tone inspired by Hims and Ro.

2. Personas

  1. New visitors (majority)
    • Arrive via search engines or campaigns.
    • Looking for a trustworthy online consultation for a prescription treatment.
    • Need to understand the steps clearly: consultation, approval, and delivery.
    • Sensitive to legitimacy, price transparency, and doctor credibility.
  2. Returning users
    • Have purchased before or used another treatment category.
    • Expect consistency, efficiency, and reliability.
    • Want reassurance that they'll receive the same service quality and delivery times.

3. Success Indicators

  • +20% increase in conversion from RX category page → start consultation.
  • +10% improvement in conversion from RX category page → paid order.
  • Maintain page bounce rate under 15%.

4. Competitor Inspiration

  • Hims
  • Ro

Both present their treatments in a clean, trustworthy layout, with strong credibility signals (doctors, delivery, reviews) and clear CTAs that guide users through a fast, digital prescription flow.
