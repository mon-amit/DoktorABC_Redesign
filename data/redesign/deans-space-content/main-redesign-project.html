Scope

Perform a facelift and design refresh for the website to make it more attractive and reliable to customers → Improve conversion and overall website performance.

The Challenge

Balancing a modern design refresh with proven high performing elements, all within our available resource timeline.

Strategic Goals

  • Build Trust: Consistent professional design, Clear, page-specific USPs and new trust banners (Data Privacy, Reviews, Medical Board, Press).
  • Simplify User Navigation: Redesign site architecture to guide users to their treatment with zero friction.
  • Launch the OTC E-commerce Engine: Build a full "online store" (Homepage, Category, Product) Moving from a lead generation strategy → profitability
  • Data-Driven Design: Implement full event mapping (Mixpanel, GA4) for data-backed decisions.

Success Metrics

Primary KPIs

  • +30% Increase in Conversion – Start Consultation
    Measure the uplift in users who begin the consultation flow compared to the current baseline.
  • +20% Increase in Conversion – Paid Order
    Track the increase in the percentage of users completing a paid order after redesign launch.

OTC Growth

  • +20% Increase in Average Order Value (AOV)
    Measure the rise in the average order value for OTC purchases following the new flow and UI changes.
  • -20% Reduction in Cart Abandonment Rate
    Track the reduction in users dropping out before completing the checkout process.

Site Performance & Health

  • Google PageSpeed Score ≥ 90
    Maintain a PageSpeed Insights score of 90 or above across all major landing and transactional pages.
