Operating Principles

The values that guide our product culture

‚ù§Ô∏è User Obsession
We start with the customer problem, not the solution. We validate hypotheses before we build and ship.
üìä Data > Opinion

ü§ù Disagree & Commit

Escalation Paths

When and how to escalate blockers

1
Level 1: Squad Sync (Immediately)
Trigger: Blocker within the squad (e.g., waiting on design, dev hurdle).
Action: PM and Tech Lead meet immediately to adjust scope or resources.
2
Level 2: Product TL (1-2 Hours / 24 Hours)
Trigger: Cross-squad dependency or unresolvable resource conflict.
Action: Raise in "Weekly Product Meeting" or direct Slack channel for arbitration.
3
Level 3: Leadership/Exec (Within 72 Hours)
Trigger: Strategic misalignment, budget requirement, or major risk.
Action: PM prepares 1-pager; Product TL presents to David/Management.
