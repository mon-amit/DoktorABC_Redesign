Persona Analysis
FTB (First Time Buyers)
Want to compare prices, seek validation from friends/family
Strategy: Thank You page (post-payment) + confirmation emails. Do NOT interrupt the funnel.
RP (Repeat Purchasers)
Already trust the platform, likely to recommend to others
Strategy: Order history, reorder pages, post-delivery follow-up emails
Influencer-types
Want to share full cart to show what they use/profit from
Strategy: Cart page "Share your cart", order confirmation with full cart summary

Context-Aware Message Templates

Different placements = different user intent. Messages should match context.

Product Page (browsing)
"Check out this product on DoktorABC!"
Thank You (post-purchase)
"I just ordered from DoktorABC - you might like it too!"
OTC Pickup/Shipping
"Can you pick up my order? Here are the details:"
Cart Share (influencers)
"Here's what I'm getting from DoktorABC:"
Unique Value Proposition
