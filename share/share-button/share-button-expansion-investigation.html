Expand the Share Button beyond product pages to additional touchpoints (funnel Thank You, OTC, reorder surfaces, cart share for influencer-type users).

Leverage word-of-mouth by making sharing frictionless and context-aware, without relying on referral incentives.

Persona Analysis

FTB - First Time Buyers

Motivation: compare prices and get help from friends or family. Behavior: still deciding and may need validation.

  • Do not interrupt the funnel - avoid hard stops and avoid forcing a WhatsApp exit mid-decision
  • Place on Thank You page post-payment
  • Also add to post-purchase confirmation emails

RP - Repeat Purchasers

Motivation: trust already established. Behavior: comfortable recommending after positive experience.

  • Order history and reorder surfaces
  • Post-delivery follow-up emails
  • Product pages they browse

Influencer-type users

Motivation: share full cart and what they use. Behavior: active sharers and wants visibility.

  • Cart page - "Share your cart"
  • Order confirmation with full cart summary
  • Account or profile area

Key insight
Post-purchase moments (Thank You, confirmation email, post-delivery) have the highest share intent because the user has already committed and is in a positive state.
Best practices checklist:
  • Visible but not intrusive
  • Limit to 3 to 5 relevant platforms
  • Mobile: native share sheet (already implemented)
  • Desktop: custom modal (already implemented)
  • Do not interrupt conversion flow

Existing Implementation (Dean's Work)

Status
Share button is live on product pages (launched Dec 9, 2025).
Current features:
  • Mobile: Native OS share sheet via navigator.share
  • Desktop: Custom modal (Facebook, WhatsApp, X, Reddit, Email, Copy Link)
  • UTM tracking: ?utm_source=share_button
  • Mixpanel events: Share Page Loaded, Button Share Clicked
  • Open Graph meta tags for link previews (product name, DoktorABC logo)
Current message template
Hi, I found this product on DoktorABC, I think you might be interested! [Product Name] [Link to Product]
Scope: RX, OTC, and Cannabis product pages in all countries.

Context-Aware Message Templates

Principle: different placements mean different intent. The copy should match the moment.

Unique Value Proposition

Why share without incentives
People share when it solves a problem or makes them look good - not just for discounts.
Core insight:
  • FTB: price comparison and validation
  • OTC: logistics coordination ("pick up my order")
  • RP: authentic recommendation after satisfaction
  • Influencer-type: visibility, cart flex, social proof
User need How share helps Compared to referral programs
Compare prices Share for advice and validation No discount needed, just decision support
Coordinate logistics Share details for pickup or shipping handoff Solves a real problem, not a paid behavior
Social proof Share after a good experience Authentic recommendation beats paid referral
Influence Share cart as content Brand exposure without affiliate costs
Differentiators:
  • Context-aware messaging
  • Native mobile share feels like the OS, not a widget
  • No funnel interruption - post-purchase only for funnel placements
  • Practical utility for pickup coordination
